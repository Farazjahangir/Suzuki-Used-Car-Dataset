# Suzuki-Used-Car-Dataset

The dataset primarily includes Suzuki models, with their respective manufacturing years:

- Mehran: 2013 - 2019
- Alto: 2013 - 2021
- Wagon R: 2013 - 2021
- Swift: 2013 - 2021
- Cultus: 2013 - 2021

### Mileage Ranges
The mileage is categorized into 6 ranges:

1. 1 - 165,000
2. 165,001 - 330,000
3. 330,001 - 495,000
4. 495,001 - 660,000
5. 660,001 - 825,000
6. 825,001 - 990000

### Price Ranges
The price is categorized into 6 ranges:

1. 1700000 - 1009999
2. 1010000 - 1849999
3. 1850000 - 2689999
4. 2690000 - 3529999
5. 3530000 - 4369999
6. 4370000 - 5209999
